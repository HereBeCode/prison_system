Shawshank Prison Visitation Policy

Visitation rights are dependent on the "security status" of the prisoner.

The following rules are inplace within our system so that invalid visitation requests will not be filled.

1. All prisoners must satisfy requirements detailed on the visitation form.

2. High security prisoners are limited to 1 visitor every 6 months.

3. Mid security prisoners are limited to 1 visitor per month.

4. Low security prisons are limited to 1 visitor per week.
